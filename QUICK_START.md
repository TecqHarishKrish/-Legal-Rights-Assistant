## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "Failed to load" error?
1. Check if PDF files are in `data/` folder
2. Delete `db/` folder
3. Restart the app

### Slow responses?
1. Reduce "Number of sources" to 1 or 2
2. Ask shorter questions

### Chat not clearing?
- Click the "🗑️ Clear Chat" button in sidebar
